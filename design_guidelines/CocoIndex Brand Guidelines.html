These guidelines are a working document. Read them, then break the rules with intention.

01 — Foundation

A framework with a face.

CocoIndex is infrastructure for AI data — incremental, declarative, boring in all the right ways. The brand is the opposite of boring. A coconut does the heavy lifting while the engineering speaks for itself.

Warm, not cute.

The mascot is characterful but not childish. We sit closer to a folk illustration than a cartoon sticker.

Declarative on the page.

Layouts state what they are. Large type, named sections, visible structure. We write like we code.

Incremental, not loud.

Change happens at the edges. Animations nudge. Hierarchy shifts a letter or a line, never the whole page.

04 — Type

Serif for every hero. Sans for everything else.

A three-family system, used with restraint. Source Serif 4 carries every hero headline across the site — homepage, docs, examples, blog landing, and blog post titles — always at weight 400 with italic em accents in coral. Inter carries every other heading, sub, and body. JetBrains Mono handles labels, numerals, and code.

05 — Voice

Clear sentences. One coconut joke.

We write for engineers who are tired. Short sentences. Concrete verbs. Humor where it doesn't slow the reader down. The mascot is charming — the copy doesn't have to be.

Do Declare your transformation. We keep the target in sync — across code changes, data changes, and time.
Active, specific, technically honest. Tells you what the framework does, not how it feels.
Don't Unlock the power of your data with our revolutionary AI-native indexing platform!
Adjective soup. Says nothing. Engineers will close the tab.
Do Only the delta. At any scale. Production-ready on day zero.
Short. Rhythmic. Earns the claim by being specific enough to verify.
Don't Go nuts with CocoIndex — we're bananas about your data! 🥥🍌
One coconut joke is charm. Two is a theme park. Never stack fruit puns.
Direct, not blunt

Say the thing. Leave room for the reader to be smart.

Technical, not cold

A warm sentence can carry a schema. A cold sentence can't.

Concrete, not clever

"PDF in, vector out" beats "unleash the power of knowledge."

Light, not loud

One wink per page. The mascot is already doing the charm work.
